Hobbies

Tim really enjoys a few different things. Working full time he has less time than he wished to spend on "hobbies" however, he always tries to make time for hitting the gym. He finds it to be the most important of his hobbies since it helps keep him in good physical shape and also helps keep his mind sharp. When he has extra downtime he really enjoys playing video games and finding new breweries to explore with his wife Natalie.

Expertise

Tim spent 8 years in the Marine Corps as a Aviation Ordnance Technician. He spent most of his time working on weapons systems for the H-1 helicopter platform and also working on crew served weapons like the 50 caliber machine gun and the M240. He currently works at a company that builds CNC routers where he has recently moved to a full time software support role with the CAD/CAM software options they provide. In the future he genuinely hopes to be in a software development role either with web or desktop application development and currently he has no preference.
